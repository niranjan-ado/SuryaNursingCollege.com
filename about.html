Institutional Overview

Detailed information regarding the establishment, objectives, and leadership of Surya Nursing Educational College.

About the College

Surya Nursing Educational College was established in 2024. The institution operates with the dual objective of providing structured nursing education and contributing to regional manpower development in the healthcare sector.

The college offers comprehensive educational programs including Auxiliary Nurse & Midwife (ANM), General Nursing Midwifery (GNM), and a Basic B.Sc. in Nursing. The campus infrastructure is equipped with lecture halls, an auditorium, and specialized laboratories that strictly comply with the operational norms mandated by the Indian Nursing Council.

Mission, Vision & Objectives

Institutional Goal

Our Mission

To bring quality healthcare to India, closer to individuals in need. The institution strives to define excellence in healthcare and set verifiable benchmarks across the medical value chain.

Dr. Vijay Kumar

Chairman & Director

Qualifications: M.S, D.A, F.C.G.P, F.I.A.S, F.I.C.S, F.M.A.S
