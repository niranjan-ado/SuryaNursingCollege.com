How to Apply

Your step-by-step guide to joining Surya Nursing Educational College.

The Application Process

Our admission process is transparent and based on merit, as per the guidelines of the state council.

Step 1
Check Eligibility Ensure you meet the age and educational requirements.
Review courses here.
Step 2
Entrance Test Admissions are strictly based on qualifying the J.C.E.C.E.B. Entrance Test.
Step 3
Counseling Qualified candidates will be called for counseling and seat allotment.
Step 4
Final Admission Visit our campus with required documents to complete admission formalities.

Required Documents

Please prepare the following original documents along with photocopies for verification during final admission:

  • description
    J.C.E.C.E.B. Admit & Rank Card
  • school
    10th & 12th Mark Sheets
  • badge
    College/School Leaving Certificate
  • verified_user
    Character Certificate
  • pin_drop
    Domicile Certificate
  • groups
    Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • add_photo_alternate
    8 Passport-sized Photographs
  • fingerprint
    Aadhaar Card
