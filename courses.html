Our Professional Nursing Programs

INC-approved courses designed to build your skills, establish strong clinical foundations, and launch your career in healthcare.

2-Year Diploma Program

Auxiliary Nurses & Midwife (ANM)

The ANM program is the foundational course for a career in nursing, focusing on community and maternal health. Students are trained to provide essential medical care to rural and urban communities, manage health centers, and assist in childbirth.

  • school
    Eligibility Requirements 10+2 Passed (Any stream)
  • person
    Age Limit 17 to 35 Years (Female Only)
  • work
    Career Prospects Community Health Workers, Home Nurses
3-Year Diploma Program

General Nurses & Midwife (GNM)

The GNM program offers comprehensive training in general nursing, midwifery, and patient care across various settings. This course prepares students to work as registered nurses in hospitals, clinics, and community health facilities.

  • school
    Eligibility Requirements 10+2 with English (Min. 40% aggregate)
  • person
    Age Limit 17 to 35 Years
  • work
    Career Prospects Staff Nurses, ICU Nurses, Ward-in-Charge
4-Year Degree Program

B.Sc Nursing (Basic)

The B.Sc Nursing program is a professional degree that prepares graduates for leadership roles in clinical care, research, and education. The curriculum is built on a strong scientific foundation and covers advanced nursing practices.

  • school
    Eligibility Requirements 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology (Min. 45%)
  • person
    Age Limit Minimum 17 Years
  • work
    Career Prospects Nurse Educators, Clinical Specialists, Administrators
