Pre-Admission Entrance Exam Dates

All applications and examinations are conducted through the official Jharkhand Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (J.C.E.C.E.B.). Please adhere to the following deadlines.

Course Name Online Application Deadline Entrance Exam Date
Auxiliary Nurses & Midwife (ANM) 10th July 2026 27th July 2026
General Nurses & Midwife (GNM) 10th July 2026 27th July 2026
B.Sc. Nursing 11th July 2026 26th July 2026

To apply, please visit the official examination board website:

Visit jceceb.jharkhand.gov.in
