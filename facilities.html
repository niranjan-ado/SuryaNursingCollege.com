Infrastructure & Facilities

An environment built for learning, clinical excellence, and holistic growth.

WELCOME TO OUR CAMPUS

A Sprawling 5.5-Acre Learning Haven

Our college is situated on a vast, green 5.5-acre campus, providing a serene and focused atmosphere for our students. The total built-up area of 60,000 sq. ft. houses a complete ecosystem for nursing education, from administrative blocks to advanced laboratories.

We believe a great learning environment is the first step to creating great nurses. Our campus is designed to be safe, accessible, and equipped with everything a student needs to succeed.

Students learn and practice the fundamental nursing procedures, from patient hygiene to administering injections, in a controlled environment.
