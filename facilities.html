Infrastructure & Facilities

A comprehensive overview of the academic, clinical, and residential infrastructure supporting the nursing curriculum.

Campus Overview

Surya Nursing Educational College is situated on a 5.5-acre campus with a built-up area of 60,000 square feet. The spatial design provides a dedicated environment for administrative functions, academic instruction, and clinical laboratory practice.

The architectural layout complies with the statutory guidelines mandated by the Indian Nursing Council, ensuring safe, accessible, and properly equipped learning spaces for all enrolled students.

Clinical Facilities & Training

Practical clinical exposure is provided through affiliations with Surya Super Speciality Hospital and other designated government and private healthcare facilities.

Institutional Library

The central library is equipped with a comprehensive collection of nursing textbooks, reference materials, medical journals, and digital resources to support evidence-based research.

Smart Classrooms Equipped with audio-visual instructional technology to facilitate interactive lectures and presentations.

College Auditorium A high-capacity venue utilized for academic seminars, workshops, and institutional gatherings.

Specialized Nursing Laboratories

Dedicated simulation and practical spaces designed to meet INC training protocols.

Nursing Foundation Lab

Utilized for practicing fundamental patient care procedures, utilizing clinical mannequins and standard hospital equipment.

OBG & Pediatrics Lab

Equipped with infant warmers, delivery simulators, and maternal care models for comprehensive midwifery and child health training.

Pre-Clinical Science Lab

Facilitates foundational education in anatomy, physiology, and microbiology through anatomical models and microscopy.

Community Health Lab

Equipped with instructional models and field kits to prepare students for public health initiatives and community-based care.
