A WORD FROM OUR PRINCIPAL

Guiding the Future of Nursing

Dear Students,

I take this opportunity to meet you through the web and am very happy to communicate with you. I believe that apart from giving empowerment in terms of knowledge and skills, education does give us more than this.

Surya Nursing Educational College was established in the year 2024. It is approved by the Indian Nursing Council, New Delhi and Jharkhand Nurses Registration Council, Ranchi, Sido Kanhu Murmu University, Dumka, Jharkhand. It is equipped with the most modern infrastructure, qualified faculty, and excellent educational and co-curricular facilities. The goal of the College is to prepare graduates in Nursing to provide comprehensive nursing care, professional development, and good citizenship to take social responsibility.

Our mission is to bring quality healthcare to India, closer to every individual in need. Looking ahead, we are dedicated to helping India build a brighter and healthier future for all its citizens. We thank you for your belief in us.

J.V. Reddy
Professor cum Principal
