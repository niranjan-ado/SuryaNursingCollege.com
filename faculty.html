format_quote Message from the Principal

Dear Students,

Welcome to Surya Nursing Educational College. Education is a fundamental tool for empowerment, extending far beyond the acquisition of basic clinical skills.

Established in 2024, our institution operates under the approvals of the Indian Nursing Council (INC) and the Jharkhand Nurses Registration Council (JNRC), with academic affiliation to Sido Kanhu Murmu University (SKMU). The college is equipped with modern infrastructure and guided by highly qualified faculty to provide a comprehensive learning environment.

Our objective is to prepare nursing graduates capable of providing holistic patient care, engaging in ongoing professional development, and fulfilling their civic and social responsibilities in the healthcare sector.

We remain committed to expanding access to quality healthcare and establishing verifiable benchmarks in medical education. Thank you for your continued support as we work toward building a healthier future.
