A WORD FROM OUR PRINCIPAL

Guiding the Future of Nursing

It is my privilege to lead a team of such dedicated and experienced educators. Our faculty is the cornerstone of the student experience at Surya Nursing Educational College. We are committed to not just teaching the curriculum, but to nurturing the values of compassion, integrity, and excellence in every student.

Our goal is to provide personalized mentorship and hands-on guidance, ensuring that when you graduate, you are not just a qualified nurse, but a confident and capable healthcare leader ready to serve the community. We look forward to welcoming you and supporting you on your journey.

Archna Minz, Principal
