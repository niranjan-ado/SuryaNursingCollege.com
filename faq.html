Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our college and programs.

Admission to all our nursing programs (ANM, GNM, and B.Sc. Nursing) is based on qualifying the J.C.E.C.E.B. (Jharkhand Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board) Entrance Test. Candidates must meet the specific eligibility criteria for their desired course and apply through the official J.C.E.C.E.B. portal when admissions are open.

We offer three main professional nursing programs: a 2-year ANM (Auxiliary Nurses & Midwife) diploma, a 3-year GNM (General Nurses & Midwife) diploma, and a 4-year B.Sc. (Basic) Nursing degree. All courses are approved by the Indian Nursing Council (INC).

For the B.Sc. Nursing degree, candidates must have passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, & Biology (PCB) securing a minimum of 45% marks. The age for entrance is between 17 and 35 years.

Yes, Surya Nursing Educational College is affiliated with S.K.M. University (SKMU) and is fully recognized by the Jharkhand Nurses Registration Council (JNRC), ensuring our programs meet the highest academic and professional standards.

Yes, we provide secure and well-maintained hostel facilities for our students. The campus is designed to be a safe and supportive home away from home, allowing students to focus on their studies.

The fee structure varies for each program. For detailed and up-to-date information on tuition fees, hostel fees, and any other charges, please contact our admissions office directly through the details provided on our Contact page.

You can find detailed information and a link to the full syllabus for each program on our 'Courses' page. Each course section provides an overview and a direct link to the respective INC-approved curriculum.

Our graduates are prepared for a wide range of roles in the healthcare sector. ANM graduates often work as community health workers, GNM graduates as staff nurses in hospitals, and B.Sc. Nursing graduates are eligible for leadership roles as nurse educators, administrators, and clinical specialists.

Our college is built on the founder Dr. Vijay Kumar's legacy of service. We offer a sprawling 5.5-acre campus with state-of-the-art labs, smart classrooms, and a commitment to evidence-based, quality education. Our focus is not just on academics but on all-round development and character building.
