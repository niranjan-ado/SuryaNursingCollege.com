A VISION REALIZED

A Legacy of Service and Excellence

Surya Nursing Educational College is the culmination of a lifelong vision from our founder, Dr. Vijay Kumar, M.S. A well-acclaimed surgeon and philanthropist, he established this institution to address the scarcity of quality healthcare education in Santhal Pargana. This campus, founded in 2023, stands as a testament to his enduring commitment to service and excellence.
