WELCOME TO

Surya Nursing Educational College

We ensure that your time with us is a successful and rewarding one. The onus being on holistic preparation of nurses, the Surya Nursing Educational College was established in the year 2024, with the twin objective of providing quality nursing education and manpower development with the highest level of education and practice.

Surya Nursing Educational College is approved by the Indian Nursing Council, New Delhi and Jharkhand Nurses Registration Council, Ranchi, Sido Kanhu Murmu University, Dumka, Jharkhand. The spacious campus, fully equipped laboratories, lecture halls, auditorium and offices comply with the Indian Nursing Council norms. Surya Nursing educational college is providing quality of education (ANM, GNM, B.SC NURSING).

Our Goal

To contribute to the socio-economic and ethical development of the nation, by providing high-quality education through institutions that have dedicated faculty and state-of-the-art infrastructure and are capable of developing competent professionals and liberal-minded citizens.
