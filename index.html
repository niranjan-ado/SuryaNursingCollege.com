About the Institution

Surya Nursing Educational College was established in 2024 with the primary objective of providing high-quality nursing education and developing skilled healthcare professionals. The institution focuses on the holistic preparation of nurses through rigorous academic and clinical training.

Our goal is to contribute to the socio-economic and ethical development of the nation. We achieve this by cultivating competent, liberal-minded healthcare professionals through dedicated faculty and state-of-the-art infrastructure capable of meeting the highest standards of medical practice.
